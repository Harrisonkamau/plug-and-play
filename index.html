Software Development
Company

We're a software development company, creating custom business software and web applications.

We provide software development services and web application development
on a wide range of platforms. We primarily work with PHP frameworks, the Java
stack, Grails, as well as developing iOS, Android and Windows Phone apps.

Agile Development

We take an agile approach to software
development because we believe it enables
us to deliver better results, faster. We
ecognise that it’s often impossible to know
everything up front so we embrace changing
requirements.

Continous integration

Our developers take quality seriously. On
larger projects, we write a suite of automation
tests alongside the code we produce. The
suite of tests grows with the codebase and is
un whenever code changes are made. This
leads to far fewer regression defects and
ultimately enables us to deliver more
functionality in less time.

Specifications

For larger projects, our consultants have the
expertise to investigate, design and document
a technical solution that meets your business’
needs. A specification reduces project risk
and uncertainty, and provides stakeholders with a
clearer understanding of the project’s goals.

UX Development

Our graphic designers and programmers love
working together to design amazing user
experiences. This mix of creativity and logical
thought leads to beautiful, intuative
and consistent user interfaces that you and your
customers will love to explore.

Rapid prototyping

It’s always easier to get an idea what the end
product will look like if you can see something
tangible early on. We use a variety of tools to
ainstorm with you, allowing you to quickly
provide feedback and ensure everyone is on
the same page from the outset of the project.

Latest technologies

We love working with new technologies. Our
development team organise a monthly ‘geek
fest’ in which developers take turns
demonstrating new tools, languages or
frameworks they’ve been learning about. With
a wide and continually growing set of skills,
we’re confident our team can select the most
suitable technology stack for your project.

Xeus - SaaS - software development

Xeus is a Software as a Service ‘SaaS’ Google Apps CRM for small businesses. A spin off of a Plug and Play internal project we raised private equity to fund the software development.

Our Kenya software development team followed the ‘lean start-up’ methodology using Grails for the business logic, and Twitter Bootstrap, Kendo UI and other frameworks for the display layer.

Case Study: Xeus CRM

Miele - Windows 8 app development

Miele were looking to develop a Windows 8 mobile app for the professional range in their business.

Plug and Play worked with Miele to rapidly develop this software following agile development methodologies. Being on a Windows 8 the software was developed in C#.

Case Study: Miele App

SCL - Custom software development

SCL is a large and complex business with lots of moving parts. To streamline their operations they decided they needed to invest in custom software development to create a platform that achieved their goals.

Built in PHP on the CodeIgniter framework, the SCL booking system leverages Twitter Bootstrap and JQuery UI for the interface.
